ERVA ULU

Hi, I am Erva!

I am currently a research scientist at PARC. I received my PhD from Carnegie Mellon University. I was advised by Levent Burak Kara and worked in the Visual Design and Engineering Lab and Graphics Lab.

My research interests include fabrication, computational design and computer graphics. Particularly, I work on algorithms that enhance the design process for fabrication methods.
